South Africa

South Africa is a beautiful destination for safari activites. Table Mountain, one of South Africa's most iconic natural wonders, stands majestically above Cape Town, its flat-topped silhouette dominating the skyline. This colossal sandstone massif, with its distinctive plateau, rises nearly 1,100 meters (3,600 feet) above sea level. Its flat peak spans approximately 3 kilometers (2 miles) in length, providing breathtaking panoramic views of the city below, the Atlantic Ocean, and the surrounding landscapes..
